Our Mission

We aim to empower farmers by offering information and tools that make farming easier, more efficient, and sustainable. Our team of agricultural experts provides customized support for every stage of the farming process.  Major district level and block level Agromet Advisory Bulletins of the project are being uploaded every Tuesday and Friday on IMD site http://agromet.imd.gov.in and http://www.imdagrimet.gov.in .  The students are assessing the weather related data received from the weather observatory established at the SIF area and updating it daily on http://www.imdagrimet.gov.in/ and also on WhatsApp to the Meteorological Department (RMC), Lucknow. Informing is being done through Whats app group and telephone.  Weather related daily data is analyzed into weekly/monthly/yearly data.  Farmers are also given weather information through phone and newspaper.  Receiving Agromet Advisory Bulletin and their feedback to farmers through 35 WhatsApp groups and uploading it on AgroDSS site agromet.imd.gov.in. These weather based agriculture advisory service bulletins are very useful for the farmers.  Feedback of farmers is also collected through telephone and WhatsApp messages regarding profit/loss incurred through weather based agriculture advisory service bulletin.  Is disseminated through M Kisan portal and also under public private partnership. More than 6 lakh farmers of the districts of Central Uttar Pradesh are being benefited by registering them in M Kisan Portal and regularly sending text SMS through M Kisan Portal.  District level weather forecast for next 5 days with respect to rainfall, maximum temperature, minimum temperature, wind speed, wind direction, relative humidity and cloudiness, weekly and crop specific advisories is provided to the farmers.  Success stories of farmers benefiting from the scheme are prepared.  Through the Mausam app, farmers can access weather forecast, radar images and can easily become proactive about weather events and prevent them from occurring.  With the help of Meghdoot app, farmers can know about temperature, rainfall, humidity and wind intensity and direction. It provides weather-based agricultural advice to farmers in local language for agriculture and animals according to their area.  Damini app alerts farmers within 30-40 minutes of a lightning strike.  Month wise IBF (Impact based weather forecast) is prepared and sent to IMD, RMC, Agriculture Department of 17 districts, Krishi Vigyan Kendra and verification of district wise IBF (Impact based weather forecast) is also done.  According to Pre, Post, Monsoon Season the newspaper is sent to IMD, Pune and RMC, Lucknow.  Short based weather forecast is sent to farmers through WhatsApp. Due to which farmers can save their crops/animals from weather events in time.
